A Few Good Reasons

Why Legionaries stay

From our own members' experience, over 85 years of the Legion worldwide.

1

A school for saints

Frank Duff, the Legion's founder, called it an organization that "makes saints by the bushel." Weekly practice in holiness, like practicing any craft, is how it forms members.

2

A living formation in the faith

Every weekly meeting includes the rosary, spiritual reading, and a talk from the spiritual director — steady, practical formation in Catholic life.

3

Friendship, not just duty

Legion work builds real friendships alongside spiritual growth — a community that serves side by side.

4

Just a few hours a week

Members commit roughly four hours weekly — a small, sustainable offering of time and effort in service.

5

A hand extended to the lapsed

Much of Legion work is quietly bringing fallen-away Catholics and new converts back to the faith — work with lasting meaning for both sides.

“

All the works of the Legion of Mary are only a means of leading souls to Jesus through Mary.

Servant of God Frank Duff
